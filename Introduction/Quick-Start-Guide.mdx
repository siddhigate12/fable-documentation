## Quick Start Guide

With this guide, you are well-armed to get started and create your first interactive demo in less than 5 minutes. 

- **Planning your demo**: Outline the key elements of your demo to have a compelling narrative. Lock in your goals and finalize the workflow before you start capturing the demo.
- **Capturing your demo**: Use our chrome extension to capture your demo and let Fable do the rest of the magic for you.
- **Editing your demo**: Make use of Fable’s all new editing features and get your demo in front of your audience in no time.
- **Sharing your demo**: Whether you intend to embed a Fable on a landing page or send it in the form of a link, you can publish your interactive demo and share it as you’d like.
- **Analyzing your demo**: Creating a demo is one thing, while analyzing it is another. With Fable’s full-suite analytics, stay on top of your demo insights 24/7.
